!log_variables

Restrict the steady state of some of the variables to be positive

Syntax

!log_variables
    Variable_Name, Variable_Name
    Variable_Name

Syntax with inverted list

!log_variables
    !all_but
    Variable_Name, Variable_Name
    Variable_Name
